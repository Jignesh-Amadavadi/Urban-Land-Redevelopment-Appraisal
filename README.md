# Urban Land Redevelopment Appraisal and Land Readjustment Planning

This repository presents a GIS-supported land management and urban redevelopment planning project completed as part of the **Land Management Seminar, Summer Term 2024**, at the University of Bonn.

The project focuses on the appraisal and redevelopment of an underutilized site in **Bonn-Endenich, Germany**. The objective was to evaluate the site’s potential for mixed-use development and prepare planning outputs including a structure plan, design plan, binding plan, and land readjustment plan.

## Project Overview

The selected site covers approximately **53,201 m²** and was analyzed using GIS mapping, local spatial data, Google Earth observations, and planning-based evaluation.

The redevelopment concept includes residential areas, commercial facilities, institutional uses, public green spaces, recreational areas, internal roads, footpaths, and community infrastructure.

## Objectives

- Conduct a GIS-supported site appraisal for an underutilized urban site.
- Analyze transport accessibility, nearby services, environmental constraints, and zoning limitations.
- Prepare structure, design, and binding plans for mixed-use redevelopment.
- Develop a land readjustment concept for ownership redistribution and public land allocation.
- Compare land value before and after planning.

## Methodology

The project followed these main steps:

1. **Site appraisal**  
   Analyzed site location, surrounding land uses, accessibility, topography, and development potential.

2. **Accessibility and service analysis**  
   Evaluated access to bus, tram, railway connections, supermarkets, schools, hospitals, cafés, restaurants, sports facilities, and public services.

3. **Constraint analysis**  
   Considered zoning limitations, nearby institutional areas, residential surroundings, private farms, and environmental areas such as Messdorf Fields.

4. **Planning concept development**  
   Prepared structure, design, and binding plans showing land-use allocation, road layout, green spaces, housing types, and public facilities.

5. **Land readjustment and valuation**  
   Calculated land redistribution, public land requirements, ownership shares, compensation logic, and land value changes.

## Planning Outputs

The project includes the following outputs:

- **Structure Plan** – overall land-use framework and development concept.
- **Design Plan** – detailed layout of housing, roads, green spaces, kindergarten, playground, commercial areas, and public facilities.
- **Binding Plan** – regulatory planning representation with building zones, roads, public/private areas, and planning symbols.
- **Land Readjustment Plan** – redistribution of land among owners and public uses, including compensation calculations.
